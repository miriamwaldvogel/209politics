General Guidelines.

Sewing classes last 3 months 5 days a week and 5 hours a day the space is open at least for 6 hours to allow the student to exercise by herself !
They are given by an appointed teacher, the students in turn help the teacher !
Each student is provided with a sewing machine which she will be able to buy by microcredit if she is promoted !
Sewing and stationery supplies are provided!
The students receive for each day a written method! which has been improved and completed over the years!
and an introduction to marketing.

20 candidates, carefully selected, from 20 to 35 of age.
Motivated to learn sewing as a profession!
We chose muslim and christians candidates and for the last 4 courses we have had 2 ladies from leprosy families.
An assessment is given at the end of the course! a certicate is given to the alumni!


Descriptif du cours

Cours de 3 mois  5 jours par semaine et5 heures par jour. Une méthode traduite en Indonésien et révisée etcomplétée régulièrement est distribuée quotidiennement.
Chaquecandidates a une machine à coudre qu’elle pourra acheter à microcreditainsi que des fournitures de papeterie et de couture. 


20 candidates sont séléctionnées nous essayons d’avoir des chrétiennes et des musulmanes.
depuis les 4 derniers cours 2 candidates de familles de lépreux sont incluses.
Elles doivent être motivées pour apprendre la couture dans un but professionnel.
Chaque candidate est évaluées à la fin du cours et reçoit un certificat si elle est promue!



Criteria for the selection of candidates


Women from 20 to 35 years of age, from different backgrounds(culture religion). We have 2 students who come from leprosy families.

Having received no previous  professional training.

Interested in learning how to sew for a living.

Able to read and write and to use the four basic operations.

Motivation to learn and practice and ultimately use their training as a profession!


The selection should be made by someone who knows well our rules and goals. The selection should be finished one month before the beginning of classes. And individual interview is compulsory. It involves:

1. social living conditions.

2. level of general education and level in sewing (beginner, intermediate, advanced).

3. motivation to lear and to use sewing to improve their living conditions.
